Jazz Bands

Different Band Compositions

Jazz has been a huge part of American history and has had many evolutions through the years.

Combos

Generally a much smaller group of 3 to 8 individuals. The composition generally includes a bare minimum of Piano, Drums, Upright Bass, and one or more horns ( Trumpet, Trombone, or one of the Saxophones)

Big Bands

A Big Band is composed of 14 to 18 musicians with a rhythm section, brass section and woodwinds. The brass is divided into Trumpets and Trombones, while the woodwinds are saxophones or occassionally clarinets. The rhythm section is composed of a drum set, an upright bass and piano, guitar, or both.

Soloists and Duets

Pianist often do solo work as they have the technical ability to play so many notes at once and other chordals instruments such as guitars can do it. Duets can occur on occassion, though they are less common. Normally a piano acompaniment to a leading instrument.
